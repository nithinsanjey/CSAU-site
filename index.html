Our Club

Computer Society of Anna University

Ramanujan Computing Center, Anna University, Chennai - 25

The Computer Society of Anna University is the oldest and largest technical society of College of Engineering, Guindy. It was originally started to foster innovation and creativity among the students in the field of Computer Science and Information Technology. Over the years, it has seen a multitude of constructive changes and has now attained the status of being the most accepted society among the student's circle in terms of exhibiting and sharing the knowledge of innovative cutting-edge technology. The most celebrated organization of the campus today, the CSAU aims high in terms of quality and success.

The CSAU provides activities galore to the student community testing their skills in various arenas and a treasure trove of opportunities in the most exciting ways possible. To list a few, EUREKA - the very first traditional event of the CSAU every academic year con¬ducted by the second years for the first years fresh from school, a real ice-breaker; BRAIN SCAN - an extremely exciting event with puzzles, math and logic enough to get any student’s brain whizzing into action

Online Programming Contest

Are you someone who takes deep pleasure and supreme satisfaction in turning caffeine into code? Then this is the ultimate platform to flaunt your coding skills. Monthly Programming contests are held on platofrms like Spoj, Codechef and Hackerrank to hone your talents and polish your skills as you race up the ladder to becoming the Ninja of Codes.

Sparks

Sparks, the fairy of festivities is an annual semitech interschool competition conducted by the first year and second year volunteers of the Computer Society of Anna University. Armed with the goal to amaze budding school students with equal measures of tech and fun,Sparks casts an almost feverish aura for a full day in the campus grounds! With exciting events like Quiz, Impromptu, Innovate, Web Weave, Dumb C, Speakathon and Mr. and Miss. SPARKS on the list, SPARKS aims at encouraging the students to take up the challenge of engineering solutions to a different level altogether.

Magazine cover Design Contest

"Design is not just what it looks like and feels like. Design is how it works-Steve Jobs”

In a quest to identify the Photoshop Nazi of the campus, the Magazine cover design contest is held every year. Calling all designers and artists to unleash their designing skills and give colors to their dreams.

Brain Scan

An event to tease and tickle your grey cells, Brain Scan tests the participants in their logical, lateral thinking and general aptitude skills. In a world apart from the mundane bookish knowledge, the event pumps up your cognitive skills.

Onsite Programming Contests

An event to provide the coding geeks of the college a platform to code their way to glory. Tackle the avalanche of questions that you are bombarded with and battle it out with the best in business as you prove your mettle in this Battle of Codes!
